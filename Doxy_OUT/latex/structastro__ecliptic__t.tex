\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Ecliptic angular and Cartesian coordinates. 

Coordinates of a celestial body as seen from the center of the Sun (heliocentric), oriented with respect to the plane of the Earth\textquotesingle{}s orbit around the Sun (the ecliptic).
