\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Information about a transit of Mercury or Venus, as seen from the Earth. 

Returned by Astronomy\+\_\+\+Search\+Transit or Astronomy\+\_\+\+Next\+Transit to report information about a transit of Mercury or Venus. A transit is when Mercury or Venus passes between the Sun and Earth so that the other planet is seen in silhouette against the Sun.

The {\ttfamily start} field reports the moment in time when the planet first becomes visible against the Sun in its background. The {\ttfamily peak} field reports when the planet is most aligned with the Sun, as seen from the Earth. The {\ttfamily finish} field reports the last moment when the planet is visible against the Sun in its background.

The calculations are performed from the point of view of a geocentric observer.
